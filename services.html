Motor Insurance

Drive Confident. Insure Smart

Protect your vehicle and your peace of mind with our comprehensive Motor Insurance.

Ordinance: This covers liability to third parties for bodily injuries only.

Third-Party Liability Insurance: Covers injury, death, or property damage caused to others.

Third-Party, Fire, and Theft Insurance: Covers third-party liabilities plus damage to your vehicle due to fire or theft.

Comprehensive Insurance: Covers damage to your own vehicle (accidents, fire, natural disasters, theft) *and* third-party liabilities.
